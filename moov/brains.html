Stefan Krause

Turning a big Wheel

A driving force in the world of EVs and broad-scale mobility, Stefan wants to reshape and create sustainable last-mile urban logistics. Stefan was Co-founder and CEO of US EV maker Canoo and is a former CFO at BMW and Deutsche Bank.

He's a man with a drive for big change, willing to stand up for what he believes in, no matter what.

Dr Angelika Berger-Sodian

4-Wheels

There are few people better qualified to work on a start-up like ours than Angelika. As an organization builder at the forefront of automotive transformation in China and Europe, she’s held senior leadership roles in Magna Steyr and more recently NIO.

Angelika has more than an eye on the future of mobility; she lives and breathes it.

Bruno Lambert

Reinventing the Wheel

An established technical and business leader in e-mobility, Bruno contributed to the first wave of Chinese EVs as President of Magna Steyr in Asia. Then as Global President of Borgward Automotive, he developed the first car manufacturer to be acquired by a mobility company.

Now he’s on a mission to create vehicles which can deliver their full potential.

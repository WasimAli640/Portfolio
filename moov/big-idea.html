The problem

Everyone loves the ease of online shopping, but it puts huge pressure on a transport system that’s struggling to cope.

Last-mile delivery drivers keep the world turning, yet today they’re stuck working in vehicles designed for investors and Instagram, not for them.

The result is inefficient deliveries, unhappy drivers, increasing emissions and worsening climate change.

OUR OPPORTUNITY

The electric revolution means things are already changing. But moving to electric alone is not enough. We need to take things even further, think even bigger, think even smarter.

If we rework the system itself, by making it more intelligent and productive, we can have a huge impact.

OUR VISION

Our vision is a world where last-mile delivery works for people and planet.

We’re rethinking urban deliveries to increase efficiency, boost productivity and improve the lives of drivers, all while minimizing waste and lifetime emissions for our planet. It’s time everyone enjoys deliveries like never before, with a system that’s fast, fair and sustainable.

OUR SOLUTIONS

We don’t just talk about changing last-mile deliveries, we’re making innovative solutions available now, and at scale.

We believe happy drivers make great deliveries. Which is why our range of smart electric vehicles, systems and tools focuses on productive technology, well-being and ease of use.

Low ownership costs, better use of assets, and one-stop-shop services make adopting our innovations a no-brainer.

We are MOOV

Delivering for people and planet.
